How Perceva Works

Perceva uses a combination of AI vision, real-time data processing, and audio feedback to help visually impaired users navigate safely and independently.

Capture Your Surroundings

Use your smart glasses or laptop camera to capture your surroundings.

AI-Powered Analysis

Our advanced AI algorithms analyze the captured images to identify obstacles, pathways, and points of interest.

Real-Time Guidance

Receive real-time audio guidance through your smart glasses or headphones, ensuring you stay on course.

Safe and Confident Navigation

Perceva your environment safely and confidently, knowing NaviSight is there to assist you every step of the way.
